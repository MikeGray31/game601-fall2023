My games from GAME 601 - Game Prototyping at American University in Fall 2023

Game Modification: Profiteer
A modification for the card game, "War", Profiteer is a single-player card game where the player tries to keep a war going as long as possible in order to profit off of it as much as possible.
PLAY
(Midterm) Non-Digital Game: Royal Lock
Royal Lock is a 2 player cooperative card game. Players must work together to create get any combination of 3 Kings, Queens, and Jacks in a row before the game gets 3 empty spaces in a row.
PLAY
Interactive Fiction: Getting Inside
A quick game made in Inform. The player just has to get inside their own house for dinner.
PLAY
Hypertext Fiction: A Quick Escape
A Twine game where the player wakes up in a jail cell in the middle of the night and must find a way to escape.
PLAY
Visual Novel: A Short Dream
A renpy game where the player navigates a dream.
PLAY
Programming with p5.js: Circles in 3D
A cool animation in p5.js where I got circle s to give the illusion of depth by having their size change as they move back and forth across the screen.

(Final) Digital Game: Untitled Space Boss
